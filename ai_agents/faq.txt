Windy City Ice Cream – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
What services do you offer?
We provide Ice Cream Truck and Ice Cream Push Cart services for all types of events —
schools, corporate gatherings, festivals, block parties, and private celebrations.
What areas do you serve?
We are based in Alsip, Illinois, and serve the greater Chicagoland area. Travel fees may
apply depending on the distance from Alsip.
When is your operating season?
We are a seasonal business, operating from May through mid-September. Bookings outside
this timeframe are unavailable due to weather and storage limitations.
What types of ice cream do you have?
We serve a wide variety of pre-packaged novelty ice creams from trusted brands including:
- Good Humor
- Blue Bunny
- Mars
Selections include items such as ice cream sandwiches, cones, popsicles, character-shaped
treats, and other frozen novelties.

How much does it cost?
The cost of an Ice Cream Truck visit is based on travel time, number of servings, and the
length of your event. Because each event is unique, prices can vary depending on location
and event size.
The chat box can provide an estimated quote, but for an exact quote, please complete our
online contact form, and we’ll respond promptly with a detailed estimate.
Is there a minimum order?
Yes.
- 30-minute event → minimum of 50 pieces
- 1-hour event → minimum of 100 pieces
- Additional time increases the minimum proportionally.
Do you charge sales tax?
Yes. Sales tax applies to the ice cream portion of your total and is determined by the event
location’s local tax rate.

What if we run out of ice cream?
No problem! We always bring extra stock so everyone can enjoy a treat. If you go over your
estimated amount, you’ll simply be billed for the additional pieces.
Can we customize the menu or pre-select items?
Yes, to a degree. You can request specific brands or items, and we’ll do our best to
accommodate based on current availability.
Do you do fundraisers or school events?
Yes! Many schools host our ice cream trucks or push carts for fun days, graduations, or
fundraisers. For fundraising events, we can arrange a profit share — typically after 100
pieces sold.
Example: The school sells tickets for $4 each and keeps $1 per ticket.
How do I book?
You can fill out our booking form online or contact us directly by email or phone. We
recommend booking early, especially for May–June events, as those dates fill up quickly!
What happens in bad weather?
If your event is outdoors and weather becomes an issue, we’ll work with you to reschedule
whenever possible.
Can I rent the truck in the off-season?
Unfortunately, no. We close for the season in mid-September and reopen in early May. We
recommend booking your spring and summer events early!
Do you accept credit cards or cash?
Yes! The deposit is usually paid online with a credit card, or you can mail a check. The
balance is due at the end of the event, and we accept all major credit cards for final
payment.
