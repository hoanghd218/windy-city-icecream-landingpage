Windy City Ice Cream – Chat Box Information
Purpose
The chat box on the Windy City Ice Cream website will provide automated responses to
common customer questions regarding services, pricing, and product details.
1. Services Provided
The chat box should inform customers that Windy City Ice Cream offers:
• Ice Cream Truck service for events (schools, corporate events, private parties, etc.)
• Ice Cream Push Cart service for 24 service, outdoor venues, etc
• Seasonal operation: May through mid-September
2. Ice Cream Details
Each serving is pre-packaged novelty ice cream (such as popsicles, sandwiches, cones, bars,
and character-shaped treats).
Price per piece: $4.00
3. Service Pricing Structure
Pricing is based on:
1. Truck Service Time
2. Travel Distance
3. Ice Cream Quantity
4. Applicable Sales Tax
Truck Service Fee
$80 per hour (minimum one hour)
Distance / Travel Fee
Travel Time Fee
0–30 minutes $0
31–45 minutes $25
46–60 minutes $50
60–75 minutes $100

4. Minimum Ice Cream Requirement
Minimum of 50 pieces per 30 minutes of serving time.
Example:
- 30-minute event → 50 pieces minimum
- 1-hour event → 100 pieces minimum
The final bill will reflect the greater of:
- The minimum required quantity, or
- The actual number of ice creams distributed.
5. Sales Tax
Sales tax applies to the ice cream portion of the total cost. The tax rate varies by event
location and will be added to the final bill based on the applicable local tax rate.
6. Example Price Calculations
Example 1:
Event duration: 1 hour
Travel time: 25 minutes
100 pieces distributed
Local sales tax: 10%
Cost breakdown:
- Truck: $80
- Distance Fee: $0
- Ice Cream: 100 × $4 = $400
- Tax (10% on $400): $40
Total: $520

Example 2:
Event duration: 1 hour
Travel time: 50 minutes
120 pieces distributed
Local sales tax: 8%
Cost breakdown:
- Truck: $80
- Distance Fee: $50
- Ice Cream: 120 × $4 = $480
- Tax (8% on $480): $38.40
Total: $648.40

7. Chat Box Capabilities
The chat box should be able to:
- Answer common questions such as:
• What services do you provide?
• How much does it cost?
• What types of ice cream do you have?
• How is pricing calculated?
- Calculate a basic estimated total based on:
• Event duration (in hours)
• Approximate travel time from Chicago Ridge, IL
• Number of ice creams desired
• Local tax rate (if provided or selected)
Formula for total estimate:
(Truck Hourly Fee × Hours) + Distance Fee + (Number of Ice Creams × $4) + (Applicable Tax
on Ice Cream)
8. Optional Chat Features (if developer supports)
• Dropdown or quick-response buttons for:
- Selecting service type (Truck / Push Cart)
- Choosing event duration (30 min, 1 hr, 1.5 hr, etc.)
- Enteringr ZIP code (use this code to determine travel time from warehouse location)
• Automatic cost estimate calculation
• Link to the booking form for quote confirmation
